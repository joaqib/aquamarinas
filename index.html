Listing Description

Apartment in the exclusive Aquamarina residential complex a few meters from the sea in Denia. It has 3 bedrooms, a delightful living room, a full equipped kitchen with a separated cleaning room and a gorgeous terrace with pleasing views. On top of that, the apartment is fitted with big garden areas that contain a paddle tennis court, a swimming pool, a fantastic playground all assisted with the help of a concierge all year around. The apartment is protected at night with a fully trained security guard during the summer season. There are no intermediaries as we are the owners. The apartment is comfortable for holidays as well as for living the whole year. Low common costs. The photographies are real and you can visit the apartment without any commitment.
